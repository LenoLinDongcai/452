Project Description

A project is an independent one-time or long-term indefinite work task that people organize human, material, and financial resources through efforts and various methods, according to the relevant planning arrangements of the business model, in order to achieve the goals limited by quantity and quality indicators.

Ye Shengtao's "Ni Huanzhi" 25: "Of course, education is a project that cannot be easily ignored." Hong Shen's "Preliminary Knowledge of Drama Directors" Part 1 2: "Their styles, sizes, materials of production, methods of use, and so on are numerous projects." Qin Mu's "The Art Sea: The Core": "The vivid language and rich vocabulary seem to be a highly technical project."

Project parameters include project scope, quality, cost, time, and resources. The Project Management Institute (PMI) defines a project as a systematic effort to create unique products, services, or outcomes, as stated in its published Project Management Body of Knowledge (PMBOK). The following activities can all be referred to as a project:

1. Develop or operate a product;

2. Plan to hold large-scale events (such as planning and organizing weddings, large-scale international conferences, etc.);

3. Plan a self driving tour;

4. Consulting, development, implementation, and training of ERP

5. Establish and operate a sub brand.
